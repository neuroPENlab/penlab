## PENLab Mission

Our laboratory investigates brain-mind-body mechanisms underlying pain and affective symptoms across juvenile and adult populations, combining neuroimaging, psychophysiology, and behavioral measures.

### Active Projects

#### SPRINT — Signature for Pain Recovery IN Teens
A multisite project focused on identifying a neuroimaging signature for chronic pain in adolescents.

#### Brain Signatures of Chronic Pain and Depression
Investigating overlapping and distinct neural patterns across chronic pain, depression, and anxiety disorders.

#### Social and Affective Regulation of Pain
Studying the effects of social support, mindfulness meditation, and altruistic behavior on regulating pain and affective experiences.
