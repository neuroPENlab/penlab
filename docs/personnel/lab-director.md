## Marina López-Solà, PhD

**Principal Investigator — Pain and Emotion Neuroscience Lab**

### About

Dr. López-Solà investigates the brain mechanisms, psychological and sensory processes that underlie pain and affective suffering in chronic pain and depressed individuals.

### Education

- **PhD in Neuroscience and Medicine** — University of Barcelona
- **Advanced training** in neuroimaging and computational methods — University of Colorado Boulder

### Career Highlights

- Established independent laboratory at Cincinnati Children's Hospital (2017)
- Recipient of R01 grant funding (NIH)
- Returned to Barcelona with **Ramón y Cajal** award
- Secured competitive **RETOS** project funding
- Serves as Editor and Board Member for various scientific organizations

### Research Vision

Dr. López-Solà aims to develop a robust translational pain and affective neuroscience research program spanning Europe and the United States.
